Admission Process

Greenfield Academy welcomes students from diverse backgrounds. Our admission process is designed to ensure a good fit between the student and our school's mission and values. We look forward to guiding you through this exciting journey.

{% comment %}

Step 1: Inquiry

Contact us to learn more about Greenfield Academy and schedule a tour of our campus. Our admissions team is here to answer your questions and provide information about our programs.

Step 2: Application

Complete the application form and submit the required documents. This includes academic records, recommendation letters, and a personal statement from the student.

Step 3: Interview

Attend an interview with our admissions committee. This is an opportunity for us to get to know the student and for the student to learn more about our school community.

Step 4: Decision

After reviewing your application and interview, we will notify you of our decision. Successful applicants will receive an acceptance letter and information on the next steps.
